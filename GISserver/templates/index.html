Location information for Perpetuality.org

Entrypoints:
  • Point on map information: /v1/[long],[lat]
    Result JSON:
    • long:Longitude
    • lat: Latitude
    • solar_watts: Solar power on the surface in watts per square meter.
URL Parameters:
  • [long]: Longitude >= -180, <180
  • [lan]: Latitude >= -90, <90
